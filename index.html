Sasha Voitovych

Contact: voitovyc@mit.edu

I am a second-year PhD student at MIT IDSS / LIDS, very fortunate to be advised by Alexander (Sasha) Rakhlin. My interests are in the foundations of interactive learning. Recently, I have been working on oracle-efficient online learning and reinforcement learning for language model post-training.

During my undergrad at the University of Toronto, I was very lucky to work with Daniel Roy, Rachid Guerraoui, and Dan Alistarh.
